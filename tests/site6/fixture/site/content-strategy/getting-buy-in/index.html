Getting buy-in

You need buy-in from the top level in your organisation to improve content and workflow.

This will ensure you get the right people for the job, as well as budget and support throughout the content lifecycle.

  • Define your goal
    Set your goal and scope so you are clear about what you are selling the need for.
  • Know your stakeholders
    To tailor a buy-in pitch for content improvement you need to know exactly who your stakeholders are.
  • Ask for people
    Work out the number of people and the type of skills you need.
  • Ask for budget
    Most business owners think in terms of return on investment. Show the value of your content improvement in numbers if you can.
  • Pitch your case
    Pitch content improvement around the goal of meeting a user need.
