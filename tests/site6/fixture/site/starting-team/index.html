Starting a team

How to set up a multidisciplinary team to design, build and maintain a service.
  • Why
  • When
  • How
Why

Why do you need to work in a multidisciplinary team?

It’s exciting to start something new. It’s also a big challenge — designing services is complex.

If you can get the right roles in the team from the start you will build momentum and be able to start delivering and iterating quicker.

The team needs the right combination of roles to build services that meet the needs of users.

The people in the team also need to be open to collaborating and working in new ways.

When

When do you need to work in a multidisciplinary team?

Start working in a multidisciplinary team when you begin Discovery.

The roles and people that you have in the team may change during Alpha, Beta and Live, but the team should always be multidisciplinary.
